Buildstash can be used to distribute binaries for [any platform](/api/data/platforms) to your users.

For some platforms when distributing to users, whether via [share links](/builds-and-releases/sharing), [distribution groups](/distribution-groups), or so on, users will be guided through the installation flow for their device.
